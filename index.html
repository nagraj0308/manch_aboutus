Introducing Manch - the ultimate event management app for college students and organizers!
Manch is your one-stop solution for all your offline event needs. Whether it's college events, competitions, or workshops, Manch makes it easy to stay up-to-date with everything that's happening around you.
With Manch, you can discover new events happening in your city, register for them, and win competitions. You can also create your own events, invite participants, and manage them seamlessly.
One of the critical features of Manch is the ability to disburse certificates for events. As a participant, you can easily download your certificates and save them on your phone for easy access. Organizers can also manage and distribute certificates efficiently using the app. Certificates are stored in the form of jpg, png or pdf, jpeg in the phone.
Manch also allows you to network with other attendees, chat with organizers, and get real-time updates on event schedules and timings. You can also create your own personalized event calendar and get reminders for upcoming events.
With Manch, you can have a hassle-free event experience, whether you're a participant or an organizer.
